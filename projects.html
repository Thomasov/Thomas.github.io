Auburn Office of Accessibility

July 2014

Auburn University's Office of Accessibility C# .NET web application that I wrote early in my career as a web developer. There's not a lot to it, but I'm proud of a lot of the little things about it. It boasts a dynamic system for adding pages that the client can do easily without ever touching any code. They can also manage many other aspects of the site, such as number of links on the home page, images in the slideshow, and news articles on the home page. All of this information is stored in an SQL Server database.
